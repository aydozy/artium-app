Privacy Policy

Privacy is a right. Here's exactly what Artium collects, why, and how your data is protected.

Effective date: March 26, 2026

🔒 Short version: Artium only collects what's necessary to provide the service. Your data is never sold. Your photos are used only to generate your artwork and nothing else.

1 About Artium

Artium is an AI-powered photo-to-art application developed and operated by Ayda Nil Özyürek. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Artium iOS application.

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out at ozyurek.aydanil@gmail.com.

2 Information Collected

Only what is needed to provide the Artium service is collected:

  • Photos you upload: Images you choose to submit for artwork generation. These are processed by AI and not stored permanently after your artwork is created.
  • Usage data: Basic, anonymized information about how you use the app (e.g., which features are used) to help improve the experience.
  • Device information: Basic device type and OS version, used for technical support and compatibility.
  • In-app purchase data: Managed entirely by Apple. Artium does not directly process or store your payment information.

Your name or email address is not collected, and no account is created unless you voluntarily reach out for support.

3 How Your Information Is Used

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Generate AI artwork from your uploaded photos
  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Artium app
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Understand usage patterns to make the app better

Your photos are not used for training AI models, advertising, or any other purpose beyond generating your requested artwork.

4 Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is never sold, traded, or rented to third parties.

Limited data may be shared only with:

  • AI processing providers: To generate artwork from your photo. These providers are contractually bound to use your data only for this purpose.
  • Apple: For in-app purchases and App Store functionality, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect legal rights.

5 Data Retention

Photos you upload are used to generate your artwork and are not permanently stored on the servers. Generated artworks are saved locally on your device within the app and are only deleted if you choose to remove them or uninstall the app.

6 Children's Privacy

Artium is not directed at children under 13 years of age. Personal information from children under 13 is not knowingly collected. If you believe such information has been inadvertently collected, please reach out immediately at ozyurek.aydanil@gmail.com.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the information held about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please reach out at ozyurek.aydanil@gmail.com.

8 Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When that happens, the "Effective date" at the top of this page will be updated. Please review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
